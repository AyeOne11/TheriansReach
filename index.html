The Owl's Whisper

The Owl’s Whisper follows Eli, a restless boy in a storm-battered fishing village, who dreams of wings amid the weight of nets and salt. When a mysterious owl drops a carved stone and lures him into a storm-swept moor, Eli discovers an ancient ruin and a guardian who demands he prove his worth. As feathers tear through his skin and his senses sharpen, Eli must choose: claim his Therian owl heritage and guard the cliffs, or return to a human life that no longer fits. A tale of transformation, freedom, and the wild call of the unknown.

The Fox's Lantern

The Fox’s Lantern follows Mira, a solitary girl in snowbound Hrimvall, marked by whispers of her Therian blood. On the longest night, a black fox with glowing eyes leads her to a shimmering grove, where lost spirits beg for guidance and a lantern pulses with her fading light. Year by year, guiding them home hollows her—until she must choose: resist and fade, or surrender to the fox within. A tale of sacrifice, identity, and the fierce glow of becoming whole.
